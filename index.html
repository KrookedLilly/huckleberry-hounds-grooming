Meet Amanda

Amanda is the heart and soul of Huckleberry Hounds. As a one-woman operation, she gives every dog her complete, undivided attention. There are no assembly lines here — just one groomer, one pup, and a whole lot of love.

With a genuine passion for dogs of all shapes and sizes, Amanda has built a reputation that speaks for itself: a perfect 5.0-star rating with every single client describing her as "the best." Her approach is calm, patient, and always focused on making each dog feel safe and comfortable.

Established in 2024, Huckleberry Hounds may be new to Spokane Valley, but Amanda's dedication to quality grooming and natural products has already earned a loyal following. Every pup goes home looking sharp, smelling great, and wearing a fresh bandana.
